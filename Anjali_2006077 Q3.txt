<h1>ARRAYS</h1>
<h2>Introduction</h2>

<p>An array is a fixed size sequenced collection of elements of the same data type.It is simply a grouping of like type data.</p>
<p>In its simplest form,an array can be used to represent a list of numbers,or a list of names.</p>
<p>Some examples where the concept of an array can be used:</p>
<article>
<ol type="1">
	<li>List of temperatures recorded every hour in a day,month or a year.</li>
	<li>List of employees in an organization.</li>
	<li>List of products and their cost sold by a store.</li>
</ol>
